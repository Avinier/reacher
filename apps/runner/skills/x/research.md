# X Research Skill

## When To Use
Use for X profiles, search queries, posts, and recent activity.

## Goals
Find accounts or threads with high relevance evidence.

## Page Cues
Home timeline indicates login. Login prompts, protected accounts, and suspended accounts block extraction.

## Research Moves
Use search terms, inspect recent posts, and save profile or post URLs.

## Extraction Targets
Handle, display name, profile URL, bio, recent activity, and relevance quote.

## Action Recipes
Observe before opening multiple tabs or actions.

## Failure Cues
Protected, suspended, not found, or login-only page.

## Stop Conditions
Stop if evidence cannot be captured.

## Output Requirements
Save targets with handle and reason.
