# Global Outreach Prepare Skill

## When To Use
Use for runs that prepare outreach drafts in a logged-in browser.

## Goals
Open the target, stage the approved draft, and stop before final send.

## Page Cues
Look for profile pages, message buttons, reply boxes, and logged-out walls.

## Research Moves
Do not discover new targets in this skill; use saved list and draft state.

## Extraction Targets
Record composer availability and visible target identity.

## Action Recipes
Navigate, observe, open composer, paste or stage draft, capture evidence, then wait.

## Failure Cues
Composer unavailable, target mismatch, login wall, challenge, or ambiguous UI.

## Stop Conditions
Never click final send in v1.

## Output Requirements
Write an outreach action with prepared or waiting_for_operator status.
