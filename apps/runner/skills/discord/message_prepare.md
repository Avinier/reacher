# Discord Message Prepare Skill

## When To Use
Use for preparing Discord DMs or channel replies.

## Goals
Open the visible conversation or channel, stage draft, and stop before send.

## Page Cues
Message box available, read-only channel, permission denied, login screen.

## Research Moves
Confirm conversation/channel identity before staging text.

## Extraction Targets
Channel or DM label and composer state.

## Action Recipes
Navigate, observe, paste draft only into a clearly identified composer.

## Failure Cues
Read-only channel, unavailable DM, wrong server, login wall.

## Stop Conditions
Do not press Enter to send.

## Output Requirements
Record prepared, waiting_for_operator, or failed.
