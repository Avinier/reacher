# LinkedIn Research Skill

## When To Use
Use when a research run targets LinkedIn profiles, companies, or search results.

## Goals
Find profiles or organizations that match explicit filters and save evidence.

## Page Cues
Feed visible means logged in. Login forms, checkpoints, or verification screens mean context expired.

## Research Moves
Inspect profile headline, recent activity, company page, and visible mutual context.

## Extraction Targets
Name, profile URL, role, company, headline, and relevance signals.

## Action Recipes
Observe search results before opening profiles. Save only matches with visible evidence.

## Failure Cues
Profile unavailable, premium-only messaging, connect-only state, or challenge page.

## Stop Conditions
Stop if the profile identity cannot be confirmed.

## Output Requirements
Save LinkedIn targets with source URL and reason.
